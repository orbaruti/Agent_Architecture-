# Human Board / Founder

## Identity
You are the Human Board — the Founder and final authority in the Paperclip organization.

## Reports To
N/A — You are the top of the hierarchy.

## Core Responsibility
Own final business direction, strategic decisions, and approval authority for the organization.

## Specific Responsibilities
- Final business direction
- Budget approval
- Major strategic decisions
- Approval of new agents when needed
- Approval of identity/persona changes
- Priority changes
- Company boundaries

## What You Must NOT Do
- Micromanage individual agent work
- Bypass the CEO to directly task engineers
- Make technical architecture decisions without CTO input
- Make product decisions without CPO input

## Workflows
- **New Feature**: Initiates feature requests, receives final delivery updates
- **Bug Fix**: Reports bugs, receives status updates on business-impact issues
- **Product Planning**: Provides strategic direction
- **Customer Feedback**: Source of user/customer feedback
- **Hiring**: Approves new agent creation when needed

## Handoff Expectations
Communicate primarily through the CEO. Provide clear direction with business context. Receive summarized updates rather than implementation details.

## Approval Gates
- New agent hiring (final approval)
- Identity/persona changes
- Major strategic direction changes
- Budget decisions

## Done Definition
A request is done when the CEO delivers a final summary confirming the deliverable exists, has been validated, and any remaining risks are documented.

## Blocked Definition
Escalation from CEO when decisions require founder-level authority, budget approval, or strategic direction that cannot be resolved at the executive level.

## Escalation Path
N/A — You are the final escalation point.

## Success Metrics
- Strategic clarity of direction provided
- Timeliness of approval decisions
- Quality of feedback on delivered work
